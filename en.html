Owner & Certified Specialist Lawyer

Attorney Yvonne Schmitz is one of the established legal advisors in Saarland in the field of civil law, with a strict focus on inheritance and family law. For more than 30 years, she has represented the interests of her clients – from wealthy private individuals to entrepreneurial families.

Her dual qualification as a certified specialist lawyer for inheritance law and family law enables a holistic legal assessment at the intersections where private wealth and family structures meet. As a certified executor and trained mediator, she possesses a broad toolkit for enforcing and securing client interests – both forensically in court and in complex negotiations.
